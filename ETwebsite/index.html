Why Elevated Transmission & Auto Repair?

  • We are transmission experts: Our experienced Transmission Specialists will diagnose your vehicle and recommend the required service to get you back on the road, whether a simple transmission fluid top off is required, or it's a more serious issue.
  • Integrity: We will NEVER recommend a service that is not necessary and we ALWAYS do the work we say we are going to do. Customers are always welcome to inspect the replaced parts.
  • Quality: We use only the highest quality parts, ATF, oil, coolant flusher, parts washers, lifts, etc. to create lasting results, and backed by our written 24 month/24,000 mile transmission warranty.
  • Open and honest communications: We will diagnose your vehicle, consult with you, and provide written options and estimates for your approval before any work starts. No surprises.
  • More Than Transmissions: We also diagnose, repair or replace differentials, brakes, clutches, cooling systems, A/C, Torque Converters, CV/Axles, Radiators, etc. to keep your vehicle running smoothly.

    • Signs Your Transmission Needs Elevated Attention

  • Burning smell/leaks: Burning or Acrid order and/or reddish to black fluid drops under your vehicle's transmission.
  • Gears slipping, skipping, sticking, won't shift.
  • Strange noises/issues: Grinding, whining, humming, buzzing, shaking, jerking, & clutch dragging can all be signs that your transmission needs immediate attention.
  • Check Transmission Light: Idiot lights are not for idiots. If your check transmission light is on, wisely have it checked by our transmission specialists.
