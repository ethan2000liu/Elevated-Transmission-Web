Signs Your Transmission Needs Elevated Attention

Burning smell/leaks: Burning or Acrid order and/or reddish to black fluid drops under your vehicle's transmission..

Gears slipping, skipping, sticking, won't shift

Strange noises/issues: Grinding, whining, humming, buzzing, shaking, jerking, & clutch dragging can all be signs that your transmission needs immediate attention.

Check Transmission Light: Idiot lights are not for idiots. If your check transmission light is on, wisely have it checked by our transmission specialists.
